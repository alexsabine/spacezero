Who we are

Space Zero is a physical and relational space on the Northern California coast — a community of practice, held with care, open to whoever arrives.

About

Space Zero holds somatic experiences. Meditation, breath, shared creativity, silence, movement. The work of remembering that attention lives in a body, and that a body lives in a world.

We believe in alignment between all living systems — between people, between species, between the slow rhythms of place and the faster ones we impose on it. In an age shaped by artificial intelligence, that kind of alignment is not a technical question only; it is a lived one. We practise the lived side.

Space Zero is organised as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Volunteer-led, community-shaped, deliberately open to change.
