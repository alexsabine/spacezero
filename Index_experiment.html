Space Zero is a physical place where embodied practice meets scientific discovery and creative innovation; a place to pause, breathe, bridge and build.

Held by the beauty of the Northern Californian Coast, Space Zero offers ground for emptiness and fullness, where new possibilities emerge in the space between.

Creative Advance for Future Flourishing

Four grounds of practice

01

Cultivate Embodied Wisdom

Wisdom that lives in the body; values expressed as action. We help interdisciplinary thinkers to foster meaningful change.

02

Align as Nature

Alignment emerges, it is not imposed. Starlings in a murmuration track only their nearest neighbors, yet the flock turns as one. We practise local attention for global impact.

03

Benevolence into Technology

Kindness is a lighthouse for future flourishing. The builders of tomorrow are shaped by the ground they stand on. We tend that ground.

04

Living in Aesthetics

Each year, iceplant blooms along the cliffs, effortlessly beautiful. Beauty is a way of being alive. Through neuroaesthetics, it opens the brain to creativity and fresh perspectives.
