Space Zero sits at the Threshold
Practice

Space Zero is a place of gathered attention. The things we do here are simple — sitting together, breathing, making, listening, noticing — and the simplicity is the point.

All practices are open to any level of familiarity, and most are offered without cost. Donations support the space and the practitioners.

Offerings

What we do

— 01

Practice Centre

Somatic practices — meditation, yoga, breath, sound baths — alongside shared creative exercises and the quiet work of exploring open possibility together. The ground on which everything else stands.

— 02

Wisdom in Residence

Visiting thinkers, teachers, and practitioners sharing their work through guest lectures, facilitated discussion, and open reflection. Conversation more than performance — thinking out loud, together.

— 03

Ecosystem Collaboration

Shared sustainability and educational practice with local and global partners, including nature immersion and the simple work of being together in a place. The coast is a teacher; we pay attention.

— 04

Emergent Gatherings

A rhythm of collaborative events through the year — shaped in the moment by whoever arrives, facilitated with care, held without a fixed agenda.

And inland, the redwoods and the river. The other edge.
