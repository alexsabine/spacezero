Ways to join

Space Zero is held by the people who show up for it. Here are the current ways to do so — each at its own pace, each welcome.

Keep in touch

A quiet, occasional note — news of what is unfolding at Space Zero.

Subscribe to the Space Zero mailing list to hear about upcoming gatherings, visiting speakers, and reflections from the practice. We write when there is something worth writing about — and no more often than that.

— 02

Become a partner

An invitation to visiting philosophers, technologists, and thinkers to share the work.

Our offerings — the Practice Centre, Wisdom in Residence, Ecosystem Collaboration, and Emergent Gatherings — are held in partnership with those who bring their own lineage of practice or thought to the coast.

If you are a practitioner, researcher, or artisan whose work meets the body, the ecology, or the present question, we would welcome a conversation. The same invitation extends to organisations, studios, and collectives whose work is adjacent to ours.

We particularly welcome Visiting philosophers and contemplative teachers · technologists working at the edge of AI and ecological design · artists and writers whose practice includes the body · scientists tending to complex living systems.
To propose a residency, talk, or collaboration, write to [partnerships@...]
— 03

Donate

Space Zero is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Philanthropic support keeps the doors open.

Donations support the space itself, the practitioners who hold it, and the offerings that run within it. Because Space Zero is organised as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, contributions from US donors may be tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

Gifts of every size help. In-kind contributions, volunteer time, and introductions to aligned philanthropists are also welcomed — sometimes more than money.
