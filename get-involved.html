02

Become a partner

An invitation to visiting philosophers, technologists, and thinkers to share the work.

Our offerings (Lab Zero, The Emergent Community, Creativity in Residence, and Open Ground) are held in partnership with those who bring their own lineage of practice or thought to the coast.

If you are a practitioner, researcher, or artisan whose work meets the body, the ecology, or the present question, we would welcome a conversation. The same invitation extends to organisations, studios, and collectives whose work is adjacent to ours.

We particularly welcome Visiting philosophers and contemplative teachers · technologists working at the edge of AI and ecological design · artists and writers whose practice includes the body · scientists tending to complex living systems · researchers and university affiliates · NGOs and aligned nonprofits exploring sponsorship.
To propose a residency, talk, collaboration, or sponsorship, write to info@spacezero.net.

Donate

Space Zero is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Philanthropic support keeps the doors open.

If you are a philanthropist or an investor exploring how to support Space Zero, please write to info@spacezero.net. We would welcome a conversation about how a contribution might align with what you care about and the work we are building.

The door is open. Come however you arrive.
