Crescent City · California

Space Zero is a physical place where embodied practice meets scientific discovery and creative innovation; a place to pause, breathe, bridge and build.

Held by the beauty of the Northern Californian Coast, Space Zero offers ground for emptiness and fullness, where new possibilities emerge in the space between.

Where interdisciplinary knowledge, experience and action are co-woven into a relational weave from which true innovation can emerge.

Creative Advance for Future Flourishing

Four grounds of practice

01

Living in Aesthetics

As day softens, the ocean gathers the sun's last colours and returns them, wave by wave. Beauty is a way of being alive; through neuroaesthetics it opens the brain to creativity and fresh perspective.

02

Cultivate Embodied Wisdom

Like a redwood growing its rings, wisdom accrues in the body; values expressed as action. We help interdisciplinary thinkers to foster meaningful change.

03

Align as Nature

Alignment emerges, it is not imposed. A school of fish tracks its neighbours, yet the whole turns and folds as one. We practise local attention for global impact.

04

Benevolence into Technology

Kindness gives technology its pulse — and pulse finds pulse, two rhythms drawn into time with one another. The builders of tomorrow are shaped by the ground they stand on. We tend that ground.
