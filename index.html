Our Inspiration

We are at a critical moment in history. Fragmentation and separation across all sectors of society are calling for deep healing and integration. Space Zero is a living commitment to bridge science and spirituality, technology and nature, reason and beauty. As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, we work with both high-impact technological innovation and low-income communities. We believe the path forward lies in alignment and attunement among humans, nature, and artificial intelligence. We cultivate sources of love and creativity, and hold space for the imagination needed to build a more beautiful future. We do not walk this path alone. In partnership, in community, in trust. We create infrastructure for shared resources.

Zero is the source of infinite possibility.

Elements

Four grounds of practice

— 01

Cultivate Embodied Wisdom

Wisdom that lives in the body; values expressed as action. We hold space for the kind of knowing, like the concentric rings of a redwood — each layer a season held, the whole, a life.

— 02

Align as Nature

Alignment is not imposed; it emerges. Starlings in a murmuration each track only their seven nearest neighbors, yet the flock turns as one. We practice alignment through local attention to global impact.

— 03

Benevolence into Technology

We see kindness as the lighthouse for collective flourishing across human, AI, and nature. Through embodied compassion—for self, others, and systems—we support AI builders to transmit benevolence into the future. Like a lighthouse, technology can be grounded in care. The builders of tomorrow are shaped by the ground they stand on. We tend the ground.

— 04

Living in Aesthetics

Each year, iceplant blooms along the Battery Point cliffs, effortlessly beautiful. Beauty is a way of being alive—and through neuroaesthetics, it shapes the brain’s neuroplasticity, opening creativity and fresh perspectives.
