About

Space Zero is a not-for-profit space for somatic and embodied practice, based in Crescent City on the Northern California coast. We hold a global reach with a local presence — a place where meditation, shared creativity, and emergent gatherings enhance entrainment between all living systems.

The space is deliberately open to change.

Elements

Four grounds of practice

— 01

Cultivate Embodied Wisdom

Wisdom that lives in the body. We hold space for the kind of knowing that accumulates slowly, like the concentric rings of a redwood — each layer a season held, the whole a life.

— 02

Align as Nature

Alignment is not imposed; it emerges. Starlings in a murmuration each track only their seven nearest neighbours, yet the flock turns as one. We practise alignment as this kind of local attention.

— 03

Benevolence with Technology

A lighthouse is technology in service of life — tending a coastline, helping rather than watching. We carry the same ethic toward the tools we build and share: care first, capability second.

— 04

Living in Aesthetics

The iceplant bloom arrives each year along the Battery Point cliffs without asking permission to be beautiful. Beauty is a mode of being alive, not a reward for productivity. We practise noticing.
